Volunteering

Being involved in the community has always been important to me. I've had the opprotunity to be involved with a lot of great causes that have benefitted from my skills.

St. Pauls Hospital Community Day

My classmates and I had the opprotunity to help out St. Pauls Hospital run their Community Day on May 25. We helped set up and close down, served hotdogs, and answered questions to give everyone a great time.

TDC's Eco Club

In the 2015-2016 school year, many students from TDC got together to create the school's first Off The Grid Ecoclub. We met many times to discuss our clean energy goals, and got many things accomplished in our first year. We started a social media campaign and made several videos to support our cause. We built and maintained several vermi composts, and worked very hard to establish a garden at the back of the school for all to enjoy. We also did an energy audit to see how much power the school wasted and how we could cut back, as well as fundraising and establishing a deal with SaskPower to install solar panels.

TDC's Art Club

In the 2015-2016 school year I had the amazing opprotunity to work with TDC's art club to complete two murals in the hallway of the school. We collaborated with local artists and with eachother to design a unique mural to represent our community's roots and values. We completed the project over several weekends, putting in long hours to get the job done.
